📜 RTE & RPWD Compliance Guidelines

📘 RTE Act

  • Free and compulsory education (6–14 years).
  • Inclusive education for all children.
  • Equal opportunity and no discrimination.
  • Individualized support for each child.
  • Continuous assessment required.

♿ RPWD Act

  • Inclusive education for disabled children.
  • Reasonable accommodations in teaching.
  • Use of assistive technology.
  • IEP must be developed.
  • Skill development and independence focus.
